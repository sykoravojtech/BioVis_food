Heatmap

This heatmap shows the top 5 ingredients in each cuisine and their percentage of occurence in recipes of such cuisine.

The most used ingredients are often basic ones such as water, salt, or sugar. If we remove these basic ingredients we get more into depth of what represents the cuisines of the world. Therefore water, salt, and sugar were removed from the dataset to make space for 'real' ingredients.
